Hii,

We are a team of five dedicated Computer Science students working collaboratively on the Financial Customer Churn Prediction System with the goal of applying data analytics, machine learning, and web technologies to solve real-world business problems in the financial domain. This project focuses on predicting customer churn by analyzing customer behavior and historical data, enabling businesses to take proactive steps to improve customer retention and satisfaction. Each team member has contributed to different aspects of the project, including data preprocessing, machine learning model development, frontend design, backend integration, and documentation, helping us strengthen our technical skills, teamwork abilities, and understanding of practical industry-level applications.
